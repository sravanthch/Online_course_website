Who We Are?

Edutech is a for-profit massive open online course (MOOC) provider aimed at professional adults and students. Students take courses primarily to improve job-related skills. Some courses generate credit toward technical certification. Within a few months, 1,000 instructors had created about 2,000 courses, and EduTech had nearly 10,000 registered users.

EduTech is a platform that allows instructors to build online courses and students to learn their preferred courses.

Edutech offers an experience that rivals the real classroom,, and should prove to be a useful utility for teachers and students of all subject matters.
